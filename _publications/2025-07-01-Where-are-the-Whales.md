excerpt: 'We introduce a semi-automated, human-in-the-loop detection method to identify whales in very high-resolution satellite imagery. The method uses statistical anomaly detection to flag potential whale sightings, which are then annotated by experts, greatly reducing the area requiring inspection and achieving 90-96% recall.'
